Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Browse our products, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery address, and complete payment. Your order will be delivered within 30-45 minutes.
What are the delivery charges?
Free delivery on orders above ₹299. A delivery fee of ₹30 applies for orders below ₹299.
How can I track my order?
Go to "My Orders" section in your profile to track the real-time status of your order including preparation and delivery updates.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD).
How do I return or refund an order?
If you're not satisfied with your order, contact our support within 2 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a refund or replacement.
