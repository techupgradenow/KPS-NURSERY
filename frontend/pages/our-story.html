Our Story

Founded by Dentists, Driven by Passion

We are Dr. S. Sampathkannan and Dr. R. Priyanka Sampath, both practicing dentists since 2016. Our journey into baking began unexpectedly during the COVID lockdown.

The First Cake

For our daughter Thanvisha's 3rd birthday, with no bakeries open during lockdown, we bought a small oven and created our very first cake with love. That simple moment changed everything.

Home Kitchen to Business

We began baking for family and relatives, sharing on WhatsApp. Friends loved our cakes and requested the same for their celebrations. This encouraged us to start a home-based baking kitchen for 2-3 years.

Professional Training

We invested deeply in learning - attending advanced baking courses internationally and across major Indian cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore, mastering a wide range of techniques.

SK Bakers is Born

With continuous support from customers and family, we opened our cafe - SK Bakers. Today it's a place where passion meets perfection, offering premium wedding cakes, luxury celebration cakes, desserts, pizzas, pastas, and more.

SK Cake Academy

To share our knowledge, we launched SK Cake Academy, conducting baking courses for the past one year - helping aspiring bakers learn professional skills with confidence.
